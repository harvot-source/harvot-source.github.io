This Video feature are the Top 10 selection of Gamefowl Bloodlines. This is just based on our personal experience in gamefowl breeding. Gamefowl breeding is closely associated with cockfighting, a popular and ancient sport in many regions. 7-STAG CLEAN-UP DERBY CROWNS 3 CHAMPS

Carmona—Circle 8 GF, Haring Bastos, and White Ears ruled the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 1 of the Manila Cockers Club, Inc., the country’s cockfighting industry game-changer, recently at the Cockers Arena. Circle 8 GF of Geronimo Sona III/Engr. Ernie Baldo; Haring Bastos of Kon. Aldwin Garcia and White Ears of JB Vinluan finished with 7 points each to win the qualifiers. Runner-ups include 3 Brothers of Francis and Frank Arellano and JA RDC of Joseph Aquino. MCC invites all interested breeders to join its 2020 Cock Season Derbies, namely the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 3 until February 16; the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 4 from February 21 to 29; the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 5 from March 3 to 11; the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 6 from March 14 to 22 and the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 7 from March 26 to April 3.

MANILA, Philippines — The Manila Cockers Club, Inc., the country’s cockfighting industry game changer, concluded its 7-Stag Clean-Up Derby from Jan. 5 to 13 at the Cockers Arena by crowning three champions. Buenas Diaz of Peter Buendia, Conqueror of Roger Jamisola and 9 De Pebrero of Antonio Espinosa were named co-champions with 6.5 points each. Next were Dynamic Star RSA of Samuel M./Arnel A; Farmboys of Ruel Lizarda; JEC 11 DIAMOND of E. Aguilar; NLH GF of Nhelvin Henave; and Viva Penafrancia of Marites Zerrudo with six points each.

Sweater

Developed by Carol Nesmith, Sweater has consistently won the majority of derby fights in hard competition over the years in the bloodiest cockpits in the Philippines and Mexico. Sweater became the winningest bloodline of fighting cock not only in the Philippines but also in South America, particularly in Mexico.

Kelso

The Kelso rooster breed is one of the most classic for combat, they are very popular and can be commonly seen in cockfighting venues. They are birds that are very good at attacking both above and below their opponents. They are very intelligent roosters, which have earned their popularity as one of the best fighting breeds.


Roundhead

Every cockfighter in the Philippines from city to rural Sitios knows what roundhead gamefowl is. Roundhead is one of the most popular breeds of fighting cocks in the country and the only thing one should know about where his gamefowl came from. The bloodline is very important since there are many roundhead breeders in the country.


Hatch

Hatch fighting cocks are of the best-known and recommended American lines in fighting cocks. One of the characteristics that distinguish this rooster is its strength and the speed of its attack. So they are excellent in combat and do not tire easily. Has greater strength in floor combat.
