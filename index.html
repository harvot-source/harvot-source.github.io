7-COCK DERBY CHAMPIONS

Carmona—Circle 8 GF, Haring Bastos, and White Ears ruled the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 1 of the Manila Cockers Club, Inc., the country’s cockfighting industry game-changer, recently at the Cockers Arena. Circle 8 GF of Geronimo Sona III/Engr. Ernie Baldo; Haring Bastos of Kon. Aldwin Garcia and White Ears of JB Vinluan finished with 7 points each to win the qualifiers. Runner-ups include 3 Brothers of Francis and Frank Arellano and JA RDC of Joseph Aquino. MCC invites all interested breeders to join its 2020 Cock Season Derbies, namely the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 3 until February 16; the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 4 from February 21 to 29; the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 5 from March 3 to 11; the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 6 from March 14 to 22 and the 7-Cock Derby Qualifiers 7 from March 26 to April 3.
